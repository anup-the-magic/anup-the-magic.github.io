About me

I'm a software engineer with a decade of industry experience, currently based out of San Francisco, CA. After graduating from Caltech in 2016 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science, I worked at at Second Spectrum, a sports analytics startup in Los Angeles. In 2019, I started at Google, working on backend services for the Google Home app. In October 2024, I decided to pivot into a field I was passionate about -- game development. I've developed a love for well architectured, maintainable, and scalable systems, minimizing technical debt and optimizing for developer productivity to help push features out quickly and effortlessly.

In the time since Google, I've taught myself Unreal Engine, Unity, Godot, and Blender, building out toy projects and full fledged games to learn as much about the ecosystems as I can.
