About me

I'm a software engineer with a decade of industry experience, currently based out of San Francisco, CA. I've developed a love for well architectured, maintainable, and scalable systems, minimizing technical debt and optimizing for developer productivity to help push features out quickly and effortlessly. After graduating from Caltech with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science, I worked at at Second Spectrum, a sports analytics startup in Los Angeles. In 2019, I started at Google, working on backend services for the Google Home app. In October 2024, I decided to pivot into a field I was passionate about -- game development.

In the time since, I've taught myself Unreal Engine, Unity, and Godot, building out toy projects and full fledged games to learn as much about the ecosystems as I can.
